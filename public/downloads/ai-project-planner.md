## Section 2: Your 7-Day Plan

| Day | Your Mission | Done? |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| **Day 1** | Try it once. Don't judge it yet — just see what comes out. | ☐ |
| **Day 2** | Try again, but give the AI more detail in your prompt. | ☐ |
| **Day 3** | Compare the AI's output to how you'd normally do this task. | ☐ |
| **Day 4** | Try a variation — a different task, same tool. | ☐ |
| **Day 5** | Show someone what you've been doing. Get their honest reaction. | ☐ |
| **Day 6** | Try the same original task with a *different* AI tool. Compare. | ☐ |
| **Day 7** | Make a call — is this worth keeping in your routine? | ☐ |

> **Tip:** You don't have to spend more than 10–15 minutes a day on this.

## Section 4: What to Try Next

| Your Score | What It Means | Your Next Step |
|------------|--------------|----------------|
| **20–25** | You found a winner. | Use it consistently. Try building it into a regular habit. |
| **15–19** | Promising, but not quite there. | Experiment with more specific prompts. Give it another week. |
| **10–14** | Mixed results. | Try a completely different task, or switch AI tools. |
| **5–9** | Not the right fit — yet. | Don't give up. Pick a totally different type of task and start over. |
