1 Writing & Communication

Emails, messages, and professional writing — done right.

Decline a meeting politely
"Write a polite email declining a meeting request. I already have a conflict at that time but want to stay on good terms. Keep it short and professional."
Write a complaint letter
"Write a firm but professional complaint letter to a company about a product I received that was damaged. I want a refund or replacement. My order number is [ORDER NUMBER]."
Follow up on a job application
"Write a brief follow-up email for a job application I submitted two weeks ago. I want to express continued interest without coming across as pushy. The position is [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY NAME]."
Thank-you note after an interview
"Write a sincere thank-you note to send after a job interview. I interviewed for a [JOB TITLE] role and want to reference that we talked about [TOPIC]. Keep it under 150 words."
Ask a neighbor about noise
"Help me write a message to my neighbor asking them to keep the noise down at night. I want to be friendly and not start a conflict. Just a few sentences."
Birthday message for a coworker
"Write a warm but professional birthday message for a coworker I don't know very well. Keep it genuine and brief."
Apologize professionally by email
"Help me write an email apologizing to a client for missing a deadline. I want to acknowledge the mistake, explain briefly without making excuses, and reassure them it won't happen again."
Ask for a reference
"Write an email asking a former manager to be a professional reference for me. I want to remind them of my work and make it easy for them to say yes. Keep it short and respectful."
Respond to a negative review
"Write a professional response to a negative online review for my small business. The customer complained about [ISSUE]. I want to acknowledge their experience and invite them to contact me directly."
Cover letter opening paragraph
"Write an engaging opening paragraph for a cover letter for a [JOB TITLE] position at [COMPANY NAME]. I have [X] years of experience in [FIELD]. Make it sound confident without being arrogant."
2 Research & Learning

Use AI as your personal explainer — for anything you don't fully understand.

Explain something in plain English
"Explain [TOPIC] like I'm a complete beginner with no technical background. Use simple words and an everyday analogy to make it click."
Compare two options
"I'm trying to decide between [OPTION A] and [OPTION B]. Give me a straightforward comparison of the pros and cons of each. My main priorities are [PRIORITY 1] and [PRIORITY 2]."
Summarize a long article
"Here is an article I want you to summarize. Give me the 5 most important points in plain language: [PASTE ARTICLE TEXT]"
Help understand a medical term
"My doctor mentioned [TERM/DIAGNOSIS]. Can you explain what this means in everyday language? I'm not a medical professional — just help me understand the basics."
Research a company before an interview
"I have a job interview at [COMPANY NAME]. What are the most important things I should know about this company going in? What questions might they ask me?"
Break down a confusing document
"I'm going to paste a document that is confusing me. Please read it and explain what it's saying in simple, plain language: [PASTE DOCUMENT]"
Explain a current event simply
"Can you explain [CURRENT EVENT OR NEWS TOPIC] in simple terms? I've heard about it but don't fully understand what's going on or why it matters."
Help my kid understand a school topic
"My [AGE]-year-old is struggling to understand [SCHOOL TOPIC] for class. Can you explain it in a way that makes sense for their age, with a simple example?"
See both sides of a debate
"What are the strongest arguments on both sides of the debate about [TOPIC]? I want to understand the issue fairly before forming my own opinion."
3 Planning & Organization

From road trips to budgets — AI handles the logistics so you don't have to.

Plan a road trip
"Help me plan a [NUMBER]-day road trip from [STARTING CITY] to [DESTINATION]. Suggest a daily route with stops, and flag any must-see spots along the way."
Build a weekly meal plan
"Create a 5-day meal plan for a family of [NUMBER]. We like [CUISINE PREFERENCES] and try to avoid [DIETARY RESTRICTIONS]. Include a shopping list at the end."
Make a packing list
"Create a packing list for a [NUMBER]-day trip to [DESTINATION] in [MONTH]. The trip will include [TYPE OF ACTIVITIES, e.g., hiking, beach, business meetings]."
Build a simple budget
"Help me build a simple monthly budget. My take-home income is [AMOUNT]. My main expenses are rent ([AMOUNT]), groceries, utilities, and transportation. Help me see where my money is going and where I might save."
Create a morning routine
"Help me design a realistic morning routine for someone who has to leave the house by [TIME]. I want to include exercise, a healthy breakfast, and some quiet time. I'm not a morning person."
Plan a birthday party
"Help me plan a birthday party for a [AGE]-year-old who loves [INTERESTS]. We have a budget of around $[AMOUNT] and expect about [NUMBER] guests. Give me a theme, activity, and food ideas."
Write a to-do list from chaos
"I'm overwhelmed with everything I need to do. Here's my brain dump: [LIST EVERYTHING ON YOUR MIND]. Help me organize this into a prioritized to-do list with a reasonable order to tackle it."
Plan a home project
"I want to [HOME PROJECT, e.g., paint my living room / organize my garage]. Break it down into a simple step-by-step plan I can actually follow on weekends."
4 Creative & Fun

AI is surprisingly good at the fun stuff — gifts, toasts, date nights, and more.

Generate gift ideas
"Suggest 10 gift ideas for a [AGE]-year-old who is interested in [INTERESTS/HOBBIES]. Budget is around $[AMOUNT]. Mix practical and fun ideas."
Start a conversation with someone new
"Give me 5 interesting conversation starters I can use when meeting new people at a [TYPE OF EVENT, e.g., networking event, neighborhood cookout, wedding]. Nothing cheesy."
Write a funny wedding toast
"Help me write a short, funny wedding toast for my [RELATIONSHIP, e.g., best friend, brother]. I want to tell a quick story about [MEMORY], make the room laugh, and end on something heartfelt. Keep it under 2 minutes."
Come up with a creative date night
"Suggest 5 creative date night ideas for a couple who has been together for a while and wants to do something different. We're in [CITY/TYPE OF AREA] and our budget is around $[AMOUNT]."
Write a short story with a kid
"I want to write a short story with my [AGE]-year-old. Start us off with an exciting opening paragraph about a [ANIMAL OR CHARACTER] who discovers [SOMETHING MAGICAL OR SURPRISING]. Keep it age-appropriate and fun."
Suggest a movie or book
"Recommend 5 [movies/books] I might enjoy if I loved [TITLE YOU LIKED]. Explain in one sentence why each one is similar."
Plan a themed dinner party
"Help me plan a [THEME] dinner party for [NUMBER] people. Give me a menu, a simple decoration idea, and one activity or game that fits the theme."
Create a fun quiz for game night
"Create a 10-question trivia quiz for family game night. Include a mix of easy, medium, and hard questions about [TOPIC, e.g., 90s movies, nature, world capitals]. Include the answers at the end."
5 Work & Professional

Prep for meetings, write bios, brainstorm, and handle work tasks faster.

Prepare for a tough meeting
"I have a meeting with [PERSON/TEAM] about [TOPIC]. Help me prepare by listing the key points I should be ready to discuss and the tough questions they might ask me."
Outline a presentation
"Create an outline for a 10-minute presentation about [TOPIC] for an audience of [AUDIENCE, e.g., my team, new clients, school board]. I want it to have a clear structure with an intro, 3 main points, and a strong close."
Summarize a long email thread
"I'm going to paste an email thread. Please summarize what has been discussed and what the current action items or open questions are: [PASTE EMAIL THREAD]"
Brainstorm ideas for a project
"I need to brainstorm ideas for [PROJECT/CHALLENGE]. The goal is [GOAL]. The audience is [AUDIENCE]. Give me 10 different ideas ranging from simple to ambitious."
Write a professional bio
"Write a short professional bio for me. I'm a [JOB TITLE] with [X] years of experience in [FIELD]. I've worked on [NOTABLE ACHIEVEMENTS]. Keep it to 3-4 sentences and write it in third person."
Prepare questions for a vendor call
"I have a call with a vendor selling [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. I want to evaluate whether it's worth the cost. Give me 8 sharp questions to ask them."
Turn meeting notes into action items
"I'm going to paste rough notes from a meeting. Please organize them into a clean summary with clear action items, who owns each one, and any decisions that were made: [PASTE NOTES]"
6 Problem Solving

Stuck on a decision or a tricky situation? AI is a great thinking partner.

Make a pros and cons list
"Help me think through a decision I'm facing: [DESCRIBE THE DECISION]. List the pros and cons of each option, and flag anything I might not have considered."
Troubleshoot a problem
"I'm having a problem with [DESCRIBE ISSUE, e.g., my WiFi keeps dropping, my dishwasher isn't draining]. Walk me through the most common causes and how to check each one, starting with the simplest fixes."
Prepare for a negotiation
"I'm going to negotiate [SITUATION, e.g., my rent, a salary offer, a car price]. Give me a strategy: what to say, how to open, what to push back on, and how to walk away gracefully if needed."
Handle a difficult conversation
"I need to have a difficult conversation with [PERSON] about [ISSUE]. Help me figure out what to say. I want to stay calm, be clear, and not make it worse. Give me a script to start with."
Questions to ask a doctor
"I have a doctor's appointment about [HEALTH CONCERN]. Help me prepare a list of questions to ask so I understand my situation and options. I want to make the most of my time."
Evaluate a contract or agreement
"I'm going to paste a [TYPE OF DOCUMENT, e.g., lease agreement, service contract]. Can you summarize the key terms, flag anything that seems unusual, and highlight anything I should ask about before signing? [PASTE DOCUMENT]"
Decide between two big life choices
"I'm trying to decide between [CHOICE A] and [CHOICE B]. My priorities are [LIST YOUR PRIORITIES]. Help me think through this clearly — I don't need someone to decide for me, just help me see it more clearly."
7 Health & Wellness

From workout plans to sleep tips — practical health help, no jargon required.

Build a beginner workout plan
"Create a beginner workout plan for someone who wants to [GOAL, e.g., lose weight, build strength, just move more]. I have [X] days per week available and [X] minutes per session. No gym membership — just [EQUIPMENT YOU HAVE, e.g., no equipment, dumbbells, resistance bands]."
Find a calming breathing exercise
"Teach me a simple breathing or relaxation exercise I can do in under 5 minutes when I'm feeling anxious or overwhelmed. Walk me through it step by step."
Get tips for better sleep
"I've been struggling to fall asleep and stay asleep. Give me a list of practical, evidence-based tips for improving my sleep. Nothing complicated — things I can actually start doing tonight."
Create a healthy meal from what's in my fridge
"I have these ingredients in my fridge and pantry: [LIST WHAT YOU HAVE]. Suggest 2-3 healthy meals I can make with what's here. Keep it simple — I'm not a great cook."
Understand a nutrition label
"I'm trying to eat healthier and I'm confused by nutrition labels. Can you explain what the most important numbers are, what to watch out for, and what's mostly marketing noise?"

Pro tip: specificity is everything.

The more specific you are, the better the response. Add details like your situation, tone, audience, and desired length. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your real information before pasting. A vague prompt gets a vague answer — a detailed prompt gets something you can actually use.
