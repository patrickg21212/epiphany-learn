Your First AI Project Planner

A 7-day experiment for absolute beginners

Congratulations on completing Module 6. You earned this.

1 Pick Your Experiment

Choose one real task from your life. Keep it small and specific.

What's one task you do regularly that feels tedious?
How long does it usually take you?
What would a good result look like?
Which AI tool will you try?
2 Your 7-Day Plan

You don't have to spend more than 10–15 minutes a day on this.

Day Your Mission Done?
Day 1 Try it once. Don't judge it yet — just see what comes out.
Day 2 Try again, but give the AI more detail in your prompt.
Day 3 Compare the AI's output to how you'd normally do this task.
Day 4 Try a variation — a different task, same tool.
Day 5 Show someone what you've been doing. Get their honest reaction.
Day 6 Try the same original task with a different AI tool. Compare.
Day 7 Make a call — is this worth keeping in your routine?

4 What to Try Next

Find your score range below and see what it means.

20–25
You found a winner.
Use it consistently. Try building it into a regular habit.
15–19
Promising, but not quite there.
Experiment with more specific prompts. Give it another week.
10–14
Mixed results.
Try a completely different task, or switch AI tools.
5–9
Not the right fit — yet.
Don't give up. Pick a totally different type of task and start over.
